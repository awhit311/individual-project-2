James Gosling

BIOGRAPHY

James Arthur Gosling is often times referred to as "Dr.Java". Gosling

was born in Calgary,Alberta. He was born on May 19,1955. As of today he's 65 years old.

He is a Canadian PC researcher that was named the lead architect

behind the Java programming language. The java programming language

was created in 1995 and it is owned by Oracle. He got a Bachelor of

Science in Computer Science from the University of Calgary and he

got his masters and Ph.D. from Carnegie Mellon Univrsity.

His doctoral recommendation was named "The Algebraic Manipulation

of Constraints". While moving toward his doctorate, he made a

version out of emacs (gosmacs), and before joining Sun

Microsystems,he manufactured a multi-processor type of Unix[1]

while at Carnegie Mellon University, similarly as a couple of

compilers and mail structures. Since 1984, Gosling has been with Sun Microsystems.
